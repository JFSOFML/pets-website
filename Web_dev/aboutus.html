About Julio Figueroa

Julio Figueroa is a dedicated family man, veteran, and tech entrepreneur with a profound commitment to serving his community and empowering others. A former Special Operations soldier with ten years of experience in combat zones, Julio's service as a Night Stalker in the U.S. Army's elite 160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment (SOAR) profoundly shaped his life. He later applied his expertise to aerospace defense and government contracting, where he honed his skills in data analysis, configuration management, and strategic planning.

Today, Julio is pursuing a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence & Business Analytics, expanding his technical knowledge to fuel his entrepreneurial ventures. He co-founded Mockly AI, an innovative startup that uses AI-generated avatars to provide mock interviews, helping individuals and students improve their interviewing skills and secure career opportunities. With a clear vision for Mockly AI's future, Julio continually seeks to refine his product to meet diverse customer needs, including a foray into the Low-End market. His work is guided by a mission to support education and workforce readiness, even partnering with programs like Cristo Rey Tampa's Corporate Work Study Program.

Beyond Mockly AI, Julio is deeply invested in his roles as a Data Analyst and Data Visualization Specialist, where he tackles challenges in inventory data verification and visualization. His tech stack includes tools like Power BI, Excel, and various machine learning frameworks, which he leverages to bring clarity and efficiency to data management tasks. Julio's experience with both corporate environments and military settings gives him a unique perspective on the analytical challenges he encounters.

At home on the East Coast, Julio balances his professional life with a strong dedication to his family. A father of two young children, he enjoys reading to them at bedtime and prioritizing family bonds. His cultural roots in the Latino community, as a proud LSP Scholar, reinforce his passion for giving back and supporting future generations. As a mentor, he regularly shares his journey and insights with young people, instilling in them the resilience and vision needed to navigate life's challenges.



Website Development Process

This website was developed as part of an assignment to create a pet-themed, data-driven site with interactive elements. The goal was to provide users with engaging insights into pet-related data using modern web development technologies.

  • Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Libraries: Chart.js for data visualization
  • Design Framework: Basic styling with custom CSS and responsive elements

The project includes pages for managing data entries (CRUD functionality), visualizing data, and showcasing user-specific content related to pet insights. My objective in building this site was to create a user-friendly interface with a focus on accessibility, usability, and aesthetic appeal.



Development Experience

Creating this website was both an exciting and insightful experience. The goal was to design a pet-themed site that not only looks friendly and inviting but also provides users with interactive tools and data insights about pets. From planning the layout to implementing interactive elements, each step required careful consideration to ensure the site is both user-friendly and visually appealing.

To start, I structured the website with HTML to outline the content and used CSS to define the visual style. I chose a warm color scheme with soft beige and earthy tones to create a cozy, pet-friendly feel. For interactive features, I used JavaScript, especially for the “Manage Pets” page, which includes a mock CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) interface to manage pet data. This setup allows users to add, edit, or delete pet information in a straightforward form, simulating real-life data management.

One of the highlights of this project was working with Chart.js to create data visualizations on the “Data Analytics” page. Using this library, I generated charts to showcase trends in pet ownership and the popularity of different pet types, making the data engaging and easy to understand. The charts add a valuable dimension to the site, helping visitors gain insights into the pet world in a fun way.

Overall, this project was an opportunity to blend design, functionality, and data presentation into a cohesive experience. The site is hosted on GitHub, where all source code is available for those interested in exploring the technical details. I hope this website brings joy and useful insights to all pet lovers who visit!
